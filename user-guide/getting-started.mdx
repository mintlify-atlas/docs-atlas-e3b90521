jøsh eliminates the friction of traditional dating apps. No swiping, no profiles to build, no small talk. Just answer a few questions via text and we'll handle the rest.

## Sign up

Getting started with jøsh takes less than 5 minutes:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your phone number">
    Visit the jøsh website and enter your US phone number (+1 format). You'll receive a welcome text immediately.

    <Info>
    The welcome message: "hey! welcome to jøsh - no swiping, no small talk, no overthinking. we'll handpick a match and text the plan. first, a few quick questions."
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Respond to the onboarding questions via SMS. The system will ask you about yourself and what you're looking for in a partner. See [Onboarding](/user-guide/onboarding) for details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit photos and ID">
    Send 2+ photos (1 close-up, 1 full-body) and a photo of your driver's license for identity verification. All photos are private and only reviewed by matchmakers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    Your application will be manually reviewed by the jøsh team. You'll receive a text when you're accepted:

    <Note>
    "good news - you're accepted to jøsh! we'll text when we've found a great match for you."
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Phone number requirements

- Must be a US phone number (+1 country code)
- Format: +1 followed by 10 digits (e.g., +15551234567)
- You can only sign up once per number
- Banned numbers cannot re-register

<Warning>
If you've already signed up, you'll receive a message: "You've already signed up! Check your texts."
</Warning>

## Privacy and security

- Your phone number is never shared with matches
- Photos are only viewed by jøsh matchmakers
- Driver's license is used solely for identity verification
- Messages are relayed through jøsh — matches don't see your real number
- You can end a match at any time
