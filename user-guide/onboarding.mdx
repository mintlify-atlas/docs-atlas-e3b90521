The onboarding process collects information about you and your preferences through a conversational SMS exchange. There's no app to download — everything happens via text message.

## How it works

After you sign up, you'll receive an introductory message followed by the first question. Simply respond naturally via SMS, and the system will guide you through all 12 questions.

<Note>
The system uses AI to evaluate your answers for quality and may ask follow-up questions to get more detailed responses.
</Note>

## The 12 questions

Here are the questions you'll answer during onboarding:

<Accordion title="1. Gender and sexuality">
**Question**: "how do you describe your gender and sexuality?"

**Purpose**: Understanding your identity helps us match you with compatible people.

**Example responses**:
- "I'm a straight woman"
- "Non-binary, pansexual"
- "Cisgender man, gay"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. Relationship intent">
**Question**: "are you looking for something long-term or a hookup? if open to both, which way are you leaning?"

**Purpose**: Ensures both people in a match have compatible expectations.

**Example responses**:
- "Looking for a long-term relationship"
- "Open to both, leaning casual"
- "Serious relationship only"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. Work and education">
**Question**: "what do you do for work or school, and what's your education background?"

**Purpose**: Helps match people with similar ambitions and lifestyles.

**Example responses**:
- "Software engineer with a CS degree from Berkeley"
- "Grad student studying neuroscience at NYU"
- "Freelance graphic designer, self-taught"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. Roots and languages">
**Question**: "where did you grow up, and what languages do you speak?"

**Purpose**: Cultural background and language can be important compatibility factors.

**Example responses**:
- "Grew up in LA, speak English and Spanish"
- "Born in Mumbai, fluent in Hindi and English"
- "Small town in Texas, English only"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="5. Kids and pets">
**Question**: "do you have kids or pets, and do you want kids in the future?"

**Purpose**: These are often dealbreakers or must-haves for people.

**Example responses**:
- "No kids, want them someday. Have a dog."
- "One kid from previous relationship, don't want more"
- "No kids, don't want any. Have two cats."
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="6. Substances and dealbreakers">
**Question**: "how do you handle drinking, smoking, and other substances? what are your dealbreakers for a partner on those?"

**Purpose**: Lifestyle compatibility around substances.

**Example responses**:
- "Social drinker, don't smoke. Dealbreaker: heavy drinking or smoking"
- "Sober, no substances. Partner needs to be sober too."
- "Drink occasionally, smoke weed sometimes. Open to most things."
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="7. Values and beliefs">
**Question**: "what are your religious and political views? do you want a partner who shares them?"

**Purpose**: Values alignment can be crucial for long-term compatibility.

**Example responses**:
- "Atheist and liberal. Want someone with similar political views."
- "Christian but not super religious. Politics don't matter much."
- "Spiritual but not religious. Want someone open-minded."
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="8. Ideal partner vibe">
**Question**: "when you picture your ideal partner, what's their general vibe: energy, ambition, humor, social style?"

**Purpose**: Personality compatibility matters as much as demographics.

**Example responses**:
- "High energy, ambitious, witty, social butterfly"
- "Calm and grounded, driven but not workaholic, dry humor, introverted"
- "Adventurous, creative, silly sense of humor, balanced social life"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="9. Age and height preferences">
**Question**: "what are your age and height preferences?"

**Purpose**: Physical and age compatibility.

**Example responses**:
- "25-32, height doesn't matter"
- "28-35, prefer taller than 5'10"
- "30-40, no height preference"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="10. Physical preferences">
**Question**: "do you have any preferences regarding ethnicity or body type?"

**Purpose**: Some people have preferences, some don't. Being honest helps create better matches.

**Example responses**:
- "No preferences on either"
- "Attracted to athletic/fit body types, ethnicity doesn't matter"
- "Open to all ethnicities and body types"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="11. Current city">
**Question**: "what city are you currently living in?"

**Purpose**: Geographic proximity for in-person dates.

**Example responses**:
- "New York City"
- "San Francisco"
- "Austin, Texas"
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="12. Anything else">
**Question**: "is there anything else you want me to know about you or what you're looking for?"

**Purpose**: Catch-all for important information not covered in other questions.

**Example responses**:
- "I'm vegetarian and prefer someone who is too"
- "I travel a lot for work, need someone flexible"
- "Looking for someone who wants to get married in the next few years"
</Accordion>

## Answer quality evaluation

The system evaluates your answers using AI to ensure they're substantive. If an answer is too short or vague, you'll receive a follow-up request asking for more detail.

<Warning>
Avoid one-word answers or extremely brief responses. The more thoughtful your answers, the better we can match you.
</Warning>

## Photo submission

After completing all questions, you'll be prompted to submit photos:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send at least 2 photos">
    "send at least 2 photos: 1 close-up and 1 full-body. private and only used by our matchmaker."
    
    Send clear, recent photos that show your face and full body. These help matchmakers understand physical compatibility.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit ID verification">
    "last step: send a photo of your driver's license to verify your identity. it's private and only used for verification."
    
    Your license is parsed to extract name, age, and height. This data is kept secure and never shared.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile structuring

Behind the scenes, your free-text responses are converted into a structured profile with badges representing your attributes and preferences. This structured data helps matchmakers quickly compare compatibility.

See [Profile Structuring](/technical/profile-structuring) for technical details.

## What happens next

Once you submit your ID photo, you'll receive a confirmation:

<Info>
"your application is in! we're reviewing it now and will text you when you're accepted."
</Info>

Your profile enters the manual review queue where matchmakers will:
- Review your photos for quality and authenticity
- Verify your ID
- Check that your profile is complete and substantive
- Make an approval decision

See [User Review](/admin-guide/user-review) to understand how admins review applications.

## Tips for great answers

- **Be specific**: Instead of "I like music," say "I'm into indie folk and go to concerts every month"
- **Be honest**: Authentic responses lead to better matches
- **Show personality**: Your vibe comes through in how you write
- **Don't overthink it**: Conversational, natural responses work best
- **Give details**: The more context, the better we can match you
